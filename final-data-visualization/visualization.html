Number of Wars over the years

What can I visualize here and how?

Map
Hello! The world map is the main component of our visualization. It's a heatmap that shows the amount of conflicts faced by a country or the amount of alliances each country has formed since 1500. The darker the colors bigger is the number they represent. You can also select a country to know who it has faced and cooperated with the most. Have I told you you can hover the countries to know more info!?
Bar Chart
The bar chart shows you the ranking of the countries which had more conflicts and alliances depending on the context of the date you are visualizing. When a country is selected on the map, the chart will show the ranking of countries the selected country has faced or cooperated with the most.
Time Series
Our time series, positioned at the bottom of the screen, lets you know how many wars happened each year over the last 500 years (518 to be precise)! By the way, when you select a country on the map, the amount of wars that country has participated in is also shown in the time series. Beaware of the legend! It will show you what country is currently selected.
How does this visualization let me interact with it?
- Conflicts and Alliances buttons: you can alternate between the context of the data (Conflicts - Alliances).
- You select a country you wanna se specific info about.
- You can hover the elements (countries, bars, dots) to get more info.
Map:
  • You can zoom in and out the map.
  • You can drag the map around.
  • Reset button: you can reset the zoom and position of the map.
Time Series:
  • Filter button: you can choose a specific period of time the time series is representing.
